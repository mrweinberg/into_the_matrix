Total Card Count: 251
1. Mechanics & Glossary
Digital
Keyword Ability
This creature can block or be blocked only by creatures with Digital.
Design Note: Functions like "Shadow". Represents the separation between the Simulation (Programs) and the Real World (Humans).
Jack-in {Cost}
Action Word
{Cost}: Transform this card. Activate only as a sorcery.
Design Note: Found on Double-Faced Cards (TDFCs). Represents Humans entering the Matrix via a hardline.
Eject {Cost}
Action Word
{Cost}: Transform this card. Activate only as a sorcery.
Design Note: The reverse of Jack-in. Represents leaving the Matrix.
Override [Quality]
Keyword Ability
When this creature enters the battlefield, sacrifice it unless you exile a [Quality] creature you control. When this creature leaves the battlefield, return the exiled card to the battlefield under its owner's control. If the exiled card was under your control continuously since the beginning of your most recent turn, this creature gains Haste until end of turn.
Design Note: A "Champion" variant used on Agents. Allows them to possess a body and attack immediately.
Energy {E}
Resource
A counter given to players. Used to pay for activated abilities or triggered effects. Represents bio-electricity and system resources.
Gun Token
Token Artifact — Equipment
Equipped creature gets +1/+1 and has first strike.
When equipped creature deals combat damage, sacrifice this Artifact.
Equip {1}
